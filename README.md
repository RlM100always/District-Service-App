# District Service Android App

District Service is an Android app that provides information about all the districts in Bangladesh. It serves as a comprehensive guide for users to learn about different districts, their demographics, tourist attractions, historical significance, and more.

## Features

- **District Information:** Get detailed information about each district, including its name, population, area, division, and notable landmarks.
- **Tourist Attractions:** Explore popular tourist attractions in each district, along with descriptions, photos, and visitor information.
- **Historical Significance:** Discover the historical significance of various districts, including notable events, monuments, and cultural heritage.
- **Local Services:** Find essential services within each district, such as hospitals, police stations, banks, and transportation options.
- **Search and Filter:** Easily search for specific districts or use filters to narrow down results based on division, population, or other criteria.
- **User Reviews and Ratings:** Share your experiences and read reviews from other users about districts and attractions.
